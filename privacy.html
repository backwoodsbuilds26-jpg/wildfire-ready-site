Privacy Policy

Wildfire-Ready · Last updated: April 19, 2026 · Effective date: April 19, 2026

The short version: Wildfire-Ready is a free public safety app. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we collect the minimum needed to show you accurate local wildfire and air quality information. If you have questions, email us at jeff@wilddeals.ca.

1. Who we are

Wildfire-Ready ("we," "us," "our") is a free mobile application that provides real-time wildfire, air quality, and preparedness information for communities in Western Canada and the United States. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Wildfire-Ready mobile application, web application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

With your permission, we collect your device's location to show you wildfires, air quality readings, and evacuation alerts relevant to where you are. Location data is used only to:

  • Display fires and air quality stations near you on the map
  • Send push notifications about wildfires within your saved locations
  • Show localized preparedness information

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device's settings. The app will continue to function, but location-specific features will be limited.

2.2 Saved Locations

You may optionally save locations within the app (for example, your home, cabin, or a family member's address) to receive notifications about wildfires near those places. Saved locations are stored in our secure database and linked to your anonymous user identifier.

2.3 Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we store a device-specific push token to deliver alerts. This token does not personally identify you.

2.4 Account Information (Optional)

Wildfire-Ready can be used anonymously. If you choose to create an account to sync saved locations across devices, we collect your email address. We do not require a real name, phone number, or other personally identifying information.

2.5 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized usage data to improve the Service, including which screens you visit, how often you open the app, and general performance metrics (such as load times and crash reports). This data does not identify you personally.

2.6 Device Information

We automatically collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to help us troubleshoot issues and ensure compatibility.

2.7 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone input, or camera data
  • We do not collect financial information, credit card data, or banking details
  • We do not track your location when the app is not in use (unless you specifically enable background location for saved-location alerts)
  • We do not collect health, biometric, or medical data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide real-time wildfire, air quality, and emergency information relevant to your location
  • Send you push notifications about wildfires and alerts affecting your saved locations
  • Improve the performance, features, and reliability of the Service
  • Diagnose technical problems and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are useful so we can prioritize improvements
  • Communicate with you about important service updates (if you have provided an email address)

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

We share limited information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services to operate the Wildfire-Ready platform:

  • Google Firebase (by Google LLC): Backend database, authentication, push notification delivery, and analytics.
  • Railway: Backend server hosting.
  • Expo / EAS: Mobile application delivery and update infrastructure.
  • Sentry: Crash and error reporting (anonymized).
  • Mapbox (or MapLibre): Map tile rendering.

These providers only access information as necessary to provide their services and are bound by their own privacy commitments.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public.

4.3 Aggregated Anonymous Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics (such as "10,000 users in BC checked fire conditions during peak fire season") publicly or with partners and funders. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Third-Party Data Sources

Wildfire-Ready displays information from public data sources including:

  • BC Wildfire Service
  • Alberta Wildfire
  • Natural Resources Canada / Canadian Wildland Fire Information System (CWFIS)
  • NASA Fire Information for Resource Management System (FIRMS)
  • Environment and Climate Change Canada (AQHI)
  • DriveBC and 511 Alberta (road conditions)
  • EmergencyInfoBC and provincial emergency management agencies

These data sources are publicly available. We display this data without tracking or associating your use of it with your identity.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service:

  • Saved locations: Until you delete them or delete your account
  • Push tokens: Until the token is invalidated by your device or you disable notifications
  • Anonymized analytics: Up to 24 months, then aggregated
  • Crash logs: Up to 90 days
  • Account information: Until you delete your account

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics, push notifications, or location tracking
  • Export your saved locations and preferences

To exercise these rights, email us at jeff@wilddeals.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Wildfire-Ready is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication for backend access
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure
  • Limiting employee access to personal data

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Users

Wildfire-Ready operates servers in Canada and the United States. If you are located outside these countries, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Canada or the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

11. Canadian Privacy Law Compliance (PIPEDA)

We comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You may contact the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you have unresolved concerns about our handling of your personal information: www.priv.gc.ca.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

13. Important Disclaimers

Wildfire-Ready is an informational tool. It is not a substitute for official emergency alerts or local authorities.

In an emergency, call 9-1-1 and follow instructions from local fire departments, emergency services, and official government sources. Wildfire data and alerts displayed in this app may be delayed, incomplete, or temporarily unavailable. Always cross-reference critical information with official sources such as BC Wildfire Service, Alberta Wildfire, Alert Ready, and local emergency management agencies.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email if you have provided one. Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
